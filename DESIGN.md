InnoExpoGl is a **non-profit science organization** dedicated to empowering students through mentorship, innovation, and collaboration.

We focus on:

- Supporting student-led ideas and research  
- Creating opportunities for learning and experimentation  
- Building a strong, collaborative innovation community
